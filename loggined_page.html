Frequently Asked Questions

Urban Solve is a platform that allows citizens to report city issues like potholes, broken streetlights, and garbage problems, ensuring a cleaner and safer environment.
You can report an issue by filling out the complaint form, marking the location on the map, and uploading photos for better clarity.
Once reported, you can track your issue's progress through your dashboard, where updates are provided as actions are taken.
Urban Solve collaborates with local municipal authorities and relevant departments to address and resolve reported problems efficiently.
Resolution times vary based on the issue type, severity, and department response time. However, most issues are addressed within a few days.
Currently, Urban Solve is available in select cities, with plans to expand nationwide based on demand and local government partnerships.
